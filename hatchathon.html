Our Hatchathons

3rd February, 2025

Introducing the West Hatch Computer Science's first Hatchathon!

The teams:

"SSS" - Saif, Sophia and Samuel
"Ulus & the Corncobblers" - Ulus, Cadan and Maxwell
"Σ" - Natalia, Tayyab and Elena

Judges: Rianna, Emily, Hannah, Aneve and Harsha

The Judges will be able to all individually award 12, 10 and 8 points repsecively to the team of their choice, and each team 12 and 10 points respectively. (they cannot vote for themselves).

This leaves a total of 156 points up for grab with maximum possible points for one team being 60 points.

Participants are allowed to plan as much as they wish before the event, but they are not allowed to start the actual development phase of the SDLC before the Hatchathon.

You can find out more about the event on our linkedin linked below. Looking forward to seeing you at our first Hatchathon!

1st: CornCobblers 68 Points, 2nd: SSS 66 Points and 3rd: Σ 52 Points.
